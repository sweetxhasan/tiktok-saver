Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

1. Information We Collect

TIK SAVE is committed to protecting your privacy. We do not collect or store any personal information from our users. The only data processed is the TikTok URL you provide for downloading purposes, which is processed in real-time and not stored on our servers.

2. How We Use Information

The TikTok URLs you submit are used solely for the purpose of processing and downloading the requested content. We do not use this information for any other purposes, and it is not shared with third parties.

3. Data Storage

We do not store any user data, including TikTok URLs, download history, or personal information. All processing happens in real-time and data is immediately discarded after the download is complete.

4. Cookies and Tracking

TIK SAVE does not use cookies or any tracking technologies. We respect your privacy and believe in providing a clean, tracking-free experience.

5. Third-Party Services

Our service interacts with TikTok's platform to fetch video data, but we do not share any user information with TikTok or any other third-party services.

6. Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access to or unauthorized alteration, disclosure, or destruction of data. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "last updated" date.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our contact page.

Your Privacy Matters

We believe in transparency and user privacy. If you have any concerns about your privacy while using our service, please don't hesitate to reach out to us.
